Organize your day as you like it - with us you can easily and quickly switch between a session on the water and your next business meeting!

Coworking Space

  • the fully equipped shared workspaces invite you to exchange ideas and connect with others.
  • fully equipped individual workspaces are available for those who need more peace and quiet.
  • all workspaces are equipped with a monitor, docking station, sockets and more.
  • stable 5G WiFi, available everywhere and already included in the rental costs.
  • at our bar you get cold drinks and snacks so that you never get hangry.
  • we also offer noise-cancelling phone boxes, fully equipped meeting rooms, as well as printers and other hardware.

Kiteboarding Club

  • our IKO-certified trainers offer lessons for all skill levels in German or English.
  • store your own kite equipment in our private storage or use our rental equipment.
  • if you have forgotten something or something breaks, you will definitely find the right replacement in our board shop.
  • compressor and cleaning station ensure quick assembly and disassembly, so you don't waste time.
  • our team ensures your safety on the water with a take-off and landing service as well as a rescue service.
  • after your session you can relax with a cool drink in our chill-out area and enjoy the view of the water.
