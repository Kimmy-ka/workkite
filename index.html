Welcome to the first Kiteboarding Club with Coworking Space - what you need and what you love in one place!

Comfort

thanks to our fully equipped workplaces and the snack bar, you are well prepared for the working day.


Network

exchange ideas about current topics, network and make new friends with like-minded people.


Flexibility

rent your workplace for the duration you need or come by spontaneously - whatever is best for you.


Activities

keep fit - whether on the surfboard or in our small outdoor gym - it's up to you. For no-wind days there are SUP's to rent.


Relax

in our cozy chill out corner you can end the successful day with a cool drink and a chat with other kiters.


Grow

learn to kitesurf, improve your skills or achieve your set goals with our IKO-certified instructors.
